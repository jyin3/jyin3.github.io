Emotions

In a world that does not always embrace unique, apps became a must for many to remain. We at Applaud pour our hearts and dedication towards the process of creation; replacing each and every „must” with „will” and passion. They say — if there is a will, there is a way. We say, if there is a will and passion, we can trace ways that evokes emotions. If there are emotions, you are on a good way for being embraced.

There's a thin line between principles and boundaries.

Many respects the principles, but only few goes beyond the boundaries at the same time.
Just like in the fashion industry there are many who follows the style — and only few who creates it.
We are not scared to say that our imagination is our only limit; regardless of the trends.
Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication - and to achieve it we have to inspire ourselves with interactions between elements in the real world, but it's easy to get lost in the process. That's why when crossing the boundaries, we use guidelines as our thin line that allows us to attach feelings to interactions, instead of creating complications in design. That makes our apps remarkable yet obvious enough to give your brand fresh wind in the sails on the ocean of similarities.
